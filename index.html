The Stella Zhang New Venture Competition

A public, citation-backed record of seventeen editions of UCI's flagship student venture competition — teams, prizes, and what became of them.

This site indexes every Grand Prize, track award, and specialty award disclosed in primary UCI Merage and UCI Samueli sources from 2009 onward. Every team has a per-year entry; every post-competition outcome is anchored to a public source.

The archive begins with the 2009 launch under the Paul Merage School of Business, follows the competition through the introduction of standard $10K/$5K track payouts in 2018, the January 2022 naming gift from Stella Zhang (FEMBA '05) and her spouse John Shen, and the March 2025 $750,000 extension that raised the prize pool past $150K.

Featured outcomes

A handful of teams have produced traceable post-competition trajectories. These are anchored to public filings, press coverage, and company announcements.

2018 · Life Sciences 1st

Mechanodontics → BRIUS Technologies

Rebranded post-NVC; HQ in Carrollton, TX. Commercial launch of the Brava hidden-braces system in June 2022. Series B of $19.5M led by View Ventures in May 2025; PitchBook reports approximately $46.2M total raised.

2013 · Grand Prize

Laser Associated Sciences → Medtronic

Acquired by Medtronic in April 2020. Continuous optical perfusion monitoring (FlowMet) integrated into Medtronic's peripheral-vascular portfolio. The cleanest documented exit in the archive.

Timeline

  1. 2009. Inaugural Merage Business Plan Competition.
  2. 2013. Laser Associated Sciences wins the Grand Prize (acquired by Medtronic in 2020).
  3. 2018. Standard track payouts reset to $10K / $5K. Mechanodontics (later BRIUS) wins Life Sciences.
  4. 2020. Pandemic-era $25K Grand Prize peak. Baby Box launches an MIE-affiliated win streak.
  5. January 2022. Stella Zhang (FEMBA '05) and John Shen announce the naming gift; competition renamed.
  6. March 2025. $750,000 five-year extension; prize pool rises past $150K.
  7. May 21, 2026. Twenty-second annual Grand Finale.

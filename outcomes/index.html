Patterns across the cohort

Read across all seventeen editions and a few shapes recur.

  1. Two clean exits to public buyers. Laser Associated Sciences to Medtronic (2020) and No Decaf Allowed to Moongoat (2022). Both happened years after the NVC win.
  2. One commercial brand rebrand under-the-radar. Mechanodontics became BRIUS Technologies in 2018. The connection is not on the public company site but is documented in Crunchbase and CB Insights.
  3. Pandemic-era wave. 2020 produced an MIE-affiliated Grand Prize streak that continues to generate the strongest non-medical operating company (123 Baby Box).
  4. Life Sciences endurance. Long FDA timelines and NIH grant pipelines keep pre-revenue companies alive for years. Sayenza, Hyperion Therapeutics, ImmunoTarget and Purist all remain operating.
  5. Consumer apps go quiet. Many Consumer Services and Social Enterprise winners produce no traceable post-competition footprint. Absence of public outcome is not the same as failure, but it is the modal pattern.
